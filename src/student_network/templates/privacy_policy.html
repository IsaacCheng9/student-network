Privacy Policy

Reconnect Privacy Policy

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before agreeing to the Reconnect terms and conditions. Reconnect is operated by Exeter University Group Software Engineering Project Group N. The Reconnect team is a group of university computer science students with the goal of working alongside the Exeter University faculty, and more importantly the student body, to develop a brand new social media connecting students through these isolating times. We wont charge anyone for the use of Reconnect.

What Sort of Data Do We Collect?

Content You Generate

We collect content, communications, usage, and other information you provide when you use the Reconnect network application. This data is stored for the purposes of usage analysis, demographic analysis, and recommendations such as relevant content.

Network and Connections

We collect information about the people you are ‘connected’ with or ‘close friends’ with and how you interact with them. This information may used for the purpose of statistics such as mutual friends, and relevant connection requests.

Information and Opinions You Provide

Occasionally, we may provide you with optional surveys asking for your opinion on certain parts of the Reconnect platform. This data is stored for us to ascertain general user opinions on our application and hopefully amend it to fix any issues or parts of the platform users seem unhappy with.

Promoting Safety, Integrity, and Security

We use the information we collect to verify authenticity of content shared from accounts and promote safety on the Reconnect platform.

Protecting Your Data

The Reconnect team wants to support you in protecting your data by helping you understand your rights according to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). The GDPR states seven main principles for the lawful processing of personal data as follows:
  • Lawfulness, Fairness and Transparency
  • Purpose Limitation
  • Data Minimisation
  • Accuracy
  • Storage Limitation
  • Integrity and Confidentiality (Security)
  • Accountability
If you have any concerns that your data associated with any (Reconnect) product is not being processed legally and securely according to these data protection laws, please contact our legal team at bg354@exeter.ac.uk.
